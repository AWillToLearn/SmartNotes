SmartNotes Take Notes, Smartly Mission Narrative Problem Taking notes and listening in class are hard to do simultaneously. You either listen, learn, or try doing both — badly. This causes a lot of frustration, especially if your professor only writes on the whiteboard, mumbles/has an accent, or doesn't share presentation slides. This leads to students needing to learn the entire course for the first time when studying for midterms or finals, causing plenty of sleepless nights and plenty of stress. Solution. To solve the issue of it being hard to take notes and listening to lectures simultaneously, SmartNotes records the lectures and takes screenshots of the white board and allows the user to drag-and-drop notes into their own local notes website. Mission Statement SmartNotes makes it simple to capture key points and make them into easy to follow descriptive notes Mission Objectives 2. 3. 4. 5. 6. Video Notes: Upload videos, transcription/screenshots made available on sidebar, and make key points available as drag-and-drop points to workspace Textbook Notes: Import Textbook pdf and use text-to-speech with built-in highlight/underline/comment features that save to read textbooks while taking notes Live Notes: Record lectures/zoom meetings live and make available key points to drag-and-drop to workspace live. Al Summary: IJpload videos/documents and receive an Al summary. An option to speak to an Al Chatbot to continue explaining topics Quiz: Upload videos/documents and uses Al to generate multiple choice quiz questions that the user can solve to check their understanding. A report should be generated explaining what the user did right and what they could improve on. Garden: Gain experience/points from actively using other features (Video Notes, Textbook Notes, Live Notes, etc) and use it to buy decorations for the user's avatar, house, and backyard (garden).
